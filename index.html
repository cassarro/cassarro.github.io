ABOUT ME

Hello, I'm a software engineering manager based in Malta. Welcome to my personal site, which I use to share ideas, best practices and anything that interests me.

My main interest is to understand how to design and optimise enterprise software systems, so as to maximize the productivity of development teams together with the business acheivements. In doing this I’ve looked to understand the patterns of good software design and also the processes that support software design. I’ve become a big fan of agile approaches and the resulting focus on evolutionary software design.

My Key Personal Skills

The leader’s job is to inspire, guide and motivate. His success is based on the team's success.

Serving my team

My primary focus is to serve the team, to empower them, to remove barriers and help them be as successful as they can be.

Keep up with technical skills

A career in software, is a lifetime commitment towards learning. This commitment does not end when one moves into management.

Working across groups

In a organization we win together or we lose together. When interacting with my teams, I keep the tone positive and don't let negativism set the mood.

Don't dictate, lead the conversation

I expose the problem and lead the team towards the answer. We build a solution together as one team, being stonger and better.

Honesty goes a long way

Honesty and transparency is a key virtue with myself and with my teams. Honesty is a way of life, not just a behavior. Keeping it paramount in your relationship will bring in more good and keep the bad stuff at bay.

Communication Skills

Understanding what other departments are doing and being able to communicate the role which technology will play. This is not about explaining technical decisions to everyone, but more about getting the buy-in from stakeholders.
